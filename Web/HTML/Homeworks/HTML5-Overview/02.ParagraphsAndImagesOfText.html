Latest news on the Rickie Lambert transfer to Liverpool.

Rickie Lambert signs for Liverpool

Today Liverpool officialy announced the transfer of the english striker.The player who left the club at the age of 15 is coming back home. He is part of the England squad for the world cup alongside with his new club mates Steven Gerrard, Jordan Henderson, Glen Johnson, Daniel Sturridge and Raheem Sterling.

This is the first major signing for Liverpool in this what is expected to be a busy summer on the transfer market, in an attemp to build a massive squad for the next season, with Champions League matches, Premier League title expectation and the domestic competitions.

First words of the new signing.

"I have always dreamt of playing for Liverpool, but I did kind of think the chance of playing for them had gone. I didn't think the chance would come."

"I know how big Liverpool are - and it means everything to me - but I know what is important; I know it's what I do on the pitch and the minutes I play. I know that's what matters, and that's what I'll be focused on."
